Popular Festivals

MassKara Festival

Bacolod City

MassKara Festival is celebrated in Bacolod City and is famous for its colorful smiling masks and energetic street dances, symbolizing the resilience, optimism, and joyful spirit of the people despite challenges.

Bangus Festival

Dagupan City

Bangus Festival is held in Dagupan City and honors the city’s thriving milkfish industry, featuring street dancing, trade fairs, and cooking competitions that highlight the importance of fishing to the local economy.

Tuna Festival

General Santos City

Tuna Festival takes place in General Santos City and celebrates the city’s identity as the Tuna Capital of the Philippines, showcasing its rich fishing industry, cultural heritage, and economic growth through parades and festive activities.
